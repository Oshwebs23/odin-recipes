Moroccan inspired slow cooked beef shin

Slow cooked Moroccan inspired beef shin with potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces of beef shin (preferably on the bone) like image above
  • 2 sticks of celery
  • 1 large brown onion
  • 2 large carrots
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 4 cloves
  • Red wine of your choice (I would recommend a cabernet sauvignon)
  • 1 large orange
  • 500ml of beef stock
  • New potatoes

Steps:

  1. First we need to make sure the meat is at room temperature before cooking, so take out of fridge for about 10 - 15 minutes.
  2. Whilst meat is warming, roughly chop the onion, celery and carrots. And chop the garlic into a paste.
  3. Stick 4 cloves into the orange.
  4. Sear the meat in a large pan. Once brown all over, take meat out of pan but do not wash the pan. We will use this pan to fry rest of the incredients.
  5. Now gently fry the onions, carrots and celery and garlic. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Now add the meat back into the pan and add the beef stock and about half a bottle of red wine.
  7. Add the orange with the cloves.
  8. Cook on a medium heat for about 2 - 4 hours until meat is tender.
  9. Serve with new potatoes and veg of choice (I personally recommend tenderstem broccoli)
